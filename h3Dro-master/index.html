Controls

Arrow keys to move

Shift key to sprint

Ctrl to fastfall in the air, use on the ground to wavebounce

In the plane levels, arrow keys control throttle and yaw while WASD controls pitch and roll

In the weird dimension level where it looks like you're at a dead end, move to the left. If someone complains that the game is impossible because of that, I'll just tell them that it was all right there in the manual.
